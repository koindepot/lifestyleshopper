Frequently Asked Questions

Beta-eshopping website makes it easy for you to purchase your favorite products from US & Europe websites - all from the comfort of your own home or place of work. All you have to do is download Share-A-Cart extension and pin it to your PC to load your cart and start shopping - that's it! You can also shop via customs order after filling the necessary details for your orders. For further guidance, visit Beta-eshopping to start your amazing shopping experience.

Yes, you do. We currently accept various payment methods like Visa, MasterCard, dollar transfers. We are constantly adding new ones over time.

There are two options available for delivery. You can pick your items at our various pickup centers across Nigeria or opt for a door-to-door delivery.

Custom order is a special order for stores not supported by the share-a-cart extension and allows you combine items from multiple stores. Click Custom Order to place your custom order. All you have to do is; copy the item URL, item name, and other necessary details regarding the product of your choice from the store you are shopping from and paste in the cells as indicated. Select your shipping type and place your order.

Our shipping fees vary based on an algorithm on size, weight, and volumetric dimensions. Other cost includes tax, duties and handling costs.

The Shipping cost may vary if two similar items are coming from different stores or location. All shipments are delivered via BETA Courier.

You can track your order status via your profile. Go to profile on the Home page, click on track order by your left and your updated order status will be displayed. BETA Courier will contact you about your delivery and let you know when to expect delivery.

Because we work with various merchants, the time it takes for an item to get to us varies. However, you can expect your item(s) within 10-15 working days depending on your specific merchant.

Yes we do, There are 2 types of returns:

1. You got the wrong item: If that is the case, BETA-eshopping will provide you with return instructions. For instruction on how to make a return please send us an email at info@beta-eshopping.com.

2. If you got the item but have changed your mind, you would be responsible for the restocking fee and the cost of shipping it back, based on the merchant’s return policy.

For safety and regulatory reasons, we do not ship items listed below;
  1. Hazardous materials/Chemicals/Radioactive material
  2. Paint/Ink and toners
  3. Medication/Prescription drugs
  4. Fire Arms/weapons/gun powder etc.
  5. Alcohol/liquid/gas
  6. Flammable substances
  7. Lighter folds and matches/Fire extinguishers
  8. Live Products including food, plants, animals
  9. Food products/Perishable goods
  10. Human remains
  11. Lithium-ion Batteries
  12. Antiques/Artworks
  13. Credit/Debit card and gift vouchers
