1. To Load a Cart

  1. Login or create an account
  2. Install the share-a-cart extension and pin to your browser
  3. Browse stores to shop.
  4. Pick items and add to cart.
  5. Click on the share-a cart icon on your extension and create CART ID as shown below:
  6. Copy your cart ID, paste and, load cart as shown below
  7. Select shipping type and click on place order.

2. Custom Orders

  1. Click on Custom Order.
  2. Copy the Item URL, item name, and other neccessary details of the item from the store you are shopping from and paste in the cells as indicated.
  3. Select your shipping type.
  4. then place order as shown below
